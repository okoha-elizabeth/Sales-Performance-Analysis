# Sales-Performance-Analysis
Excel Sales Analysis project exploring revenue, profit, expenses, and growth trend with KPI metrics and an interactive dashboard.
		
**Project Objective:**	
This project analyses the financial performance of Lizzy Electronics from 2021 to 2025, focusing on revenue trends, cost behaviour, profitability, and growth patterns.
		
**Scope of Analysis:**	
The analysis covers the following to evaluate business sustainability and financial efficiency.
•       monthly and annual revenue
•       expenses
•       net profit
•       profit margins
•       growth rates
		
**Business Questions:**
 1. How is the company's revenue changing over time?
 2.  Is the business becoming more profitable or less profitable?
 3. Are expenses growing faster than revenue?
 4. What is the company's average monthly growth rate?
 5. How efficiently is revenue being converted into profit?
		
**Tools Used:**	
•       Microsoft Excel 
•       Pivot Tables
•       Charts 	
•     Interactive Slicers
•       KPIs	
•       Conditional Formatting
•       Correlation Analysis
		
**Key Focus Areas:**	
•       Revenue and expense trends
•       Profitability analysis
•       Growth performance
•       Cost–revenue relationship
